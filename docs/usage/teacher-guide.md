# Teacher Guide

LingoPulse AI provides teachers with powerful tools to manage students and deliver high-quality language instruction.

## 👨‍🏫 Teacher Dashboard

The Teacher Dashboard is your central hub. From here, you can:
- View all your active classes.
- Monitor student enrollment.
- Track recent assignments and live sessions.

## 🏫 Managing Classes

1.  **Create a Class**: Define the class name, level, and description.
2.  **Add Students**: Use the class management interface to enroll students into your course.

## 📝 Assignments and Grading

1.  **Create Assignment**: Provide a title, description, and due date. You can also upload reference files (PDFs, images).
2.  **Monitor Submissions**: See which students have submitted their work and who is late.
3.  **Grade Work**: Review student submissions, assign a numeric grade, and provide qualitative feedback. Students will see this in their dashboards.

## 🎥 Online Sessions (Live Classes)

LingoPulse integrates with Jitsi Meet for seamless video conferencing.
1.  **Start Session**: Click "Create Online Session".
2.  **Details**: Enter the topic, start time, and duration.
3.  **Joining**: A unique room name is generated. Both you and your students can join directly from the dashboard.

## 📊 Monitoring Progress

Teachers can view student results from the global proficiency tests to understand individual strengths and weaknesses across Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking skills.
