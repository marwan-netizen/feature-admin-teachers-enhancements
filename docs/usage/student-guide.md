# Student Guide

Welcome to LingoPulse AI! This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your English proficiency assessment experience.

## 🚀 Taking Your First Test

1.  **Register/Login**: Start by creating an account or logging in.
2.  **Read Instructions**: Carefully read the test instructions provided on the dashboard.
3.  **Start the Test**: Click the "Start AI Test" button. The system will take a moment to generate a unique test just for you.
4.  **Skills Sequence**:
    - **Reading**: Read the passage and answer the multiple-choice questions.
    - **Listening**: Listen to the audio clips and select the correct answers.
    - **Writing**: Write an essay or response based on the provided topic.
    - **Speaking**: Read the given text aloud. Ensure your microphone is active.
5.  **Review Results**: Once finished, you'll be redirected to the results page where you can see your scores and detailed AI feedback.

## 💬 Using the AI Tutor

If you're unsure why you got a question wrong, use the **AI Tutor** feature:
- Click the "Explain" button next to any question.
- The AI will provide a real-time explanation of your mistake.
- You can also use the **Chatbot** in your dashboard to practice general conversation.

## 🏫 Classroom Activities

If you are enrolled in a class:
- **Dashboard**: View your upcoming assignments and deadlines.
- **Submit Assignments**: Upload your work or type your response directly into the platform.
- **Live Sessions**: Join video classes started by your teacher via the provided Jitsi links.
